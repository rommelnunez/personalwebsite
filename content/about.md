Rommel Nunez is a graduate of the School of Engineering and Applied Sciences at Columbia University where he studied Computer Science and film. Alongside his technical passions, Rommel has written, directed, and produced over 15 music videos in the last two years with his most-watched project garnering over 34,000 views on YouTube. He also directed photography for the short film, “Journeys End In Lovers Meeting” directed by Linnea Hopkins-Ekdahl. Rommel is excited to discover the intersection of technology and filmmaking as he takes the next step in his career to learn the ins and outs of the film industry. He plans to use his technical skills to revolutionize both the way that the filmmakers craft their work and the way people consume entertainment.
